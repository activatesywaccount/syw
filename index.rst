Overview
--------

You can access your Shop Your Way Mastercard account and activate your card through the secure Citi login system.  
This guide explains the steps to:

- Log in through **Syw.accountonline.com**
- Activate your card using **activate.syw.accountonline.com**
- Use the mobile app or phone activation options
- Fix common login or activation issues


Before You Start
----------------

Have the following information ready:

- Your SYW Mastercard (card number, expiration date, CVV)
- The primary cardholder’s verification info (last 4 digits of SSN)
- Access to your email or mobile device for security codes
- Your SYW/Citi username and password (if you already created one)


Option 1: Log In to Syw.accountonline.com
-----------------------------------------

#. Visit: `https://syw.accountonline.com <https://syw.accountonline.com>`_
#. Enter your **User ID**.
#. Enter your **Password**.
#. Select **Sign On**.
#. Your dashboard will load with payment options, statements, and rewards.

If you are new and do not have online access yet, choose **Register** on the login page.


Option 2: Activate Your Card Online (Fastest)
---------------------------------------------

#. Go to: `https://activate.syw.accountonline.com <https://activate.syw.accountonline.com>`_
#. Enter your card number, name on card, CVV, and last four digits of SSN.
#. Complete identity verification (security code sent to email or mobile).
#. Create your account if you are a new user.
#. Your card will activate once the confirmation message appears.


Option 3: Use the Citi Mobile App
---------------------------------

#. Download and open the **Citi Mobile App**.
#. Sign in with your Citi/SYW credentials.
#. Select your SYW Mastercard.
#. Tap **Activate** or **Finish Setup**.
#. Verify your identity if prompted and follow the steps.


Option 4: Activate by Phone
---------------------------

- Call the activation number printed on your SYW card’s sticker.
- Follow the automated instructions to verify your identity.
- Your card will activate immediately.
- You may request a representative if needed.


.. tip::
   Remove the activation sticker after completing the process so your card details remain fully visible.



Troubleshooting
---------------

**Login page not loading:**  
Clear browser cache, disable VPN, or switch to Chrome/Edge.

**Cannot sign in:**  
Reset your password using the **Forgot Password** link.

**Card not appearing in your account:**  
Log out and log back in, or ensure you are using the correct Citi profile.

**Verification code not received:**  
Check spam folder, confirm your registered phone/email, or request a new code.

**Details not matching:**  
Ensure your name, SSN, and card details exactly match your account records.

**Still having issues?**  
Contact Citi using the phone number on the back of your SYW Mastercard.
